Buddy Watch

During the Fall 2015 semester, I had the opportunity of working with Buddy Watch, a safety/companion app for walking home alone, to design their mobile app UI. My team and I went through the process of conducting interviews, creating low and mid fidelity mockups, and holding user tests. The high fidelity mockups are still in progress. As a college student, it was a really motivating experience being able to design for a product that is so relevant to my life. Walking home alone at night is a concern every college student faces, and we learned a lot while researching and designing for Buddy Watch.
